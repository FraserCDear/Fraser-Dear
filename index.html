About

From laser physics
to AI leadership

I'm a senior digital transformation leader connecting business need with data insights. My career has taken an unusual path — from a PhD in laser physics and hands-on engineering, through manufacturing and business process leadership, to the cutting edge of AI and data innovation.

I bring a hybrid of analytical and creative processes together to generate concept, development, delivery and onboarding of technical business solutions. Understanding both people and process deeply is what allows me to craft systems that genuinely serve the business.

Today at BCN, I lead our AI and Data Innovation practice — harnessing AI's power to help businesses achieve transformative change, gaining efficiencies and insights that were once unimaginable, all while maintaining ethical and secure AI practices.
